Introduction¶

Introduction

For this analysis, I selected a database on movies. This database contains films such as actors and genres. It also contains financial information about the film’s budget and revenue. These columns are named 'budget_adj' and 'revenue_adj' respectively.

We can use this data to try to answer following questions:

What is the yearly revenue change?
How was the popularity of a movie over the years?

Conclusions¶

Films with a budget of more than $ 100 million have no connection between budget and revenue. Some movie genres tend to be more lucrative, while animation and adventure genres are the best-grossing companies. There is a correlation between the highest-grossing films and the Adventure & Action genres. Also, the highest-grossing films are associated with high popularity.
